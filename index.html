About our college

We envisage GNIM as an institute of higher academic learning known for its excellence in management and professional education, research and consultancy with a holistic concern for quality of life, environment, society & ethical value.

The Mission of Greater Noida Institute of Management is to build the new generation of entrepreneurs and managers who will have capacity to manage the organisation, social responsibility through their innovation and creativity.

To help in establishing a proper environment, where students acquire knowledge and technical skills enabling them to be competitive globally. • To acquaint the students with socio-cultural and economic ethos of the nation. • To develop a balanced perception of life.
